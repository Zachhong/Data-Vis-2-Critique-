Education and Employment in Australia

This map highlights the percentage of people aged 15-24 who are fully engaged in work and/or study across Australia's states and territories. The darker the shading, the higher the percentage of engagement. This allows for a visual comparison of youth participation in education and employment across regions.
This bubble plot compares male and female employment outcomes by field of study. The size of the bubbles represents full-time employment percentages, and the colour distinguishes gender, with blue for males and pink for females. The chart helps visualise gender disparities and employment outcomes across different fields.
This line chart presents the average NAPLAN scores for Years 3, 5, 7, and 9 over time from 2012 to 2022. It highlights trends in educational performance across these years, allowing for an analysis of how performance has changed over time for each grade level.
